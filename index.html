About Kidagakash

Kidagakash Nedakh also known as Kida is the daughter of Kashekim Nedakh, the king of Atlantis during its height of power. She believes that her people are beginning to lose their sense of self in Atlantis: The Lost Empire, and begins to investigate the past to rediscover their lost civilization.
